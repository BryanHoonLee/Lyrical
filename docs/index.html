Search For Songs

Never forget lyrics to your favorite songs again.

Lyrical allows you to search for song data to any songs you want. We took care of everything going on under the hood - all you need to do is input the song name!

Record Yourself Sing

Ready, set, sing!

We bring lyrics from the Genius database straight to your mobile device! Simply tap the record button while you read from any song lyrics and sing to your hearts content!

Storage

Safely persist your recordings and favorite lyrics.

All of your wonderful recordings are stored locally onto your mobile device. You can even keep track of all of your favorite songs so you don't have to search them up again!
